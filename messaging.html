Introducing the characters

Argon: Tired most of the time. Argon deals with mild depressions which is the impression that people often get of him. He is filled with kindness, on the other hand, in which dissolves the typical disinterest and disdain towards all that is alive. His personality makes people wonder whether he possibly isn't just the apathetic type.

Aspen: Aloof most of the time. Aspen is self-driven assistent and she although charismatic, she leaves the impression of untouchable cold stone. When one has to deal with her, it turns out, nonetheless, that she is very attentive and very good in solving problems for people of all kind. Never the typical therapist, she is much more likely to disapprear for a few hours, and then come back with all of your problems taken care of.

Arvey: Cheerful most of the time. Arvey is a student a little younger than Argon. She has trouble talking to herself, and spreads all her energy around. It often leaves her exhausted and miserable with no apparent clue and out of the blue. She can never see it coming.

Jack: working most of the time. Jack is never the last one to take the initiative and he never hesitatets to do when he can do, no matter the matter. He's focused on people and covers a broad spectrum of acquitances and friends, even though it can be hard for him to stick with someone close, since he tends to be so busy that people get annoyed and misunderstand his approach as disinterest. He is a great coworker, yet he tends to miss what the people around him really crave, just as much as he tends to undervalue what he really craves himself. Just get the job done first, right?

Oliver: out with people most of the time. Oliver is one of the closest to Arvey, especially since he is never too clingy, never too present, and never really gets upset with anyone around. He is popular, hence, because he loves working with people, yet is never too carried away like Jack is. He likes things the way they are, though, which may frustrate those often around him.
